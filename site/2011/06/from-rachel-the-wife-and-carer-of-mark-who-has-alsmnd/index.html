From Rachel, the wife and carer of Mark, who has ALS/MND

Hi Patrick

You are a GENIUS !!!!!!!!!!!!

Speakbook is truly fantastic.

We are building up the pages and have a section for “Living Room” and “Bed” and are working on “Pain” and “Mood”. For Mark in the BED section – we have

  • tube change
  • suction
  • pillow
  • wipe mouth
  • injection
  • too hot
  • cold
  • cold feet
  • tv remote
  • bed remote
  • bell
  • feel sick
  • cuddle
  • love you

In the night when Mark is really tired this has proved very useful.

One other good thing about it is that its simple – my brother who comes in to help with getting Mark up has also sussed how to use it and this is brilliant.

We will keep on building up the pages and give you some more feedback but to be honest so far we can’t fault it.

WELL DONE – YOU ARE A STAR.
